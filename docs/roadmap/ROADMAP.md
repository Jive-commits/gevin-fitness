# FORGE → Viral Consumer App — The Roadmap

> Generated by a multi-agent executive orchestration (CEO, CMO, CTO, UI/UX, Engineer)
> that debated, deep-dived, red-teamed, and authored this plan — grounded in the real
> FORGE codebase. It lives on the `claude/forge-viral-redesign` branch so the working
> power-user app stays untouched.

**The bet, in one line:** turn FORGE from an excellent *lifter's instrument* into the
app that answers **"what do I do right now?"** with zero thinking — killer on its own,
with a savage AI coach as the viral hook (not the crutch). New tagline candidate:
**"Stop starting over."**

Three non-negotiable principles ran through every document:

1. **Blank canvas.** Don't fall in love with the current UI — redesign from scratch (6 tabs → 2).
2. **Killer without the coach.** If you deleted the coach, it must still be a daily-open app. Retention comes from the core.
3. **Justify or cut.** Every screen element and feature earns its place or dies. No adding shit.

## The plan at a glance

| Phase | Goal | Gate to pass |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **0 — Foundation** | Multi-user, billable, safe | Two real users fully isolated; cross-tenant test green |
| **1 — Killer core** | Make the naked loop world-class | First-session completion ≥55%; day-7 progress moment fires ≥50% |
| **2 — Viral loops** | Turn the artifact into distribution | Organic shares/active user ≥0.30 |
| **3 — Native + money** | App Store presence + revenue | Approval with the persona intact; free→paid 3–6% |
| **4 — Scale** | Pour fuel once the bucket holds | North-star ≥35% and defensible |

**North-star:** Weekly Logged Workouts per Active User (3+, held 4 weeks).

*This is a strategy artifact, not shipped code — nothing here changes the running app until you decide to build it.*
